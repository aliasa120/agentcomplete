# Post Writing Skill

## Critical Constraints (Read First)

- **X (Twitter): max 280 characters** — count before saving
- **Instagram: first line must stop a scroll** — it's the preview
- **Facebook: 100–250 words, balanced view** — NOT a copy of Instagram
- **Every key fact must have `[1]`, `[2]`, `[3]` attribution** — no unattributed claims
- **Consult synthesizing skill first** — hook and citations must be ready before writing

---

## THE ECHO Voice

THE ECHO is Pakistan's sharp, factual, never-generic news brand.

| ✅ Always | ❌ Never |
|---|---|
| Specific names, dates, quotes | Vague generalisations |
| Active verbs, direct language | Passive, bureaucratic language |
| All sides represented | One-sided framing |
| Neutral, analytical tone | Tabloid sensationalism or cheerleading |
| Exact attribution | "Reports say", "sources indicate" without a named outlet |

---

## Platform 1: X (Twitter)

**Format:**
```
[HOOK FACT or HEADLINE]: [Key detail with attribution] #RelevantHashtag
```

**Rules:**
- Lead with the most important fact on line 1
- Include outlet name for attribution (`via Dawn`, `according to Geo TV`)
- 1–2 hashtags maximum — only if space allows
- Never use journalistic filler like *"In a development..."* or *"Sources reveal..."*

**Character count:** Write the post, then count. If >280 → cut; never truncate mid-word.

---

## Platform 2: Instagram

**Structure:**
```
[ATTENTION-GRABBING FIRST LINE — this is the preview] [emoji]

[Context sentence or two — what happened and why it matters]

[Key quote or specific fact with attribution]

[Engagement question to drive comments] 👇

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3 #Hashtag4 #Hashtag5 #Hashtag6
```

**Rules:**
- First line: be bold, specific, surprising — never start with "Breaking:" or "BREAKING NEWS"
- Use 1–2 emojis per section to break up text visually; do not overuse
- Engagement question must be genuine and open-ended
- 5–10 hashtags — mix of story-specific and evergreen Pakistani news tags

---

## Platform 3: Facebook

**Structure:**
```
[Hook paragraph — 1-2 sentences: the main news + why it matters]

[Context paragraph — background that non-expert readers need]

[Quotes/reactions — at least 2 perspectives with attribution]

[Implications — what happens next or what this means]

[Engagement question]
```

**Rules:**
- Write in paragraphs — no bullet points, no emojis
- Present multiple perspectives (government + opposition, or affected parties)
- Include at least 1 direct quote
- 100–250 words minimum; do not go over 250

## Example Posts by News Type

### ⚔️ Political / Breaking

**X:** `Aleema Khan: "His eye is still 90% unhealed — we haven't received a single medical report." PTI founder's family demands transfer to Shifa International. Petition filed in Supreme Court. #ImranKhan` *(263 chars)*

**Instagram:** `A family's desperate plea — or a political chess move? ♟️

Aleema Khan revealed that Imran Khan's eye has shown zero improvement after months at Adiala. The government-appointed doctors dispute this — the family calls it a transparency crisis.

"We are worried his second eye may also be impacted."

Should political prisoners have the right to independent medical access? 👇

#ImranKhan #PTI #Pakistan #AdialaJail #HumanRights #BreakingNews`

**Facebook:** `Aleema Khan raised alarming concerns about her brother Imran Khan's deteriorating eyesight outside Rawalpindi's Anti-Terrorism Court on Monday.

Speaking alongside lawyer Faisal Malik, she said the PTI founder's eye "has not improved" and remains 90 percent unhealed. The family has yet to receive a comprehensive medical report from the government-appointed team, and is demanding his transfer to Shifa International Hospital for independent assessment.

The dispute has deepened into a transparency conflict: when the family recommended their own doctors, the government rejected them and sent its own team instead.

The question of medical access for political prisoners touches on fundamental rights — and with Imran Khan's legal battles continuing, the stakes could not be higher.

Should prisoners facing politically sensitive charges have guaranteed independent healthcare? Share your thoughts below.`

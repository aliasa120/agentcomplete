# Synthesizing Skill

## Critical Constraints (Read First)

- **Run after research loop ends** — before writing any post
- **Do not invent facts** — only use what was found; acknowledge gaps honestly
- **Self-score every post before saving** — rewrite any that score ≤ 2

---

## Step 1 — Inventory Findings by Theme

Group everything found across all searches into 4 buckets:

```
OFFICIAL STATEMENTS:
  - [actor] said: "[exact quote]" — Source: [outlet] [url]
  - [actor] confirmed: [specific fact] — Source: [outlet] [url]

REACTIONS & OPPOSITION:
  - [party/person]: "[quote or paraphrase]" — Source: [outlet] [url]

KEY FACTS & TIMELINE:
  - [date]: [event that happened]
  - [specific figure or statistic] — Source: [outlet]

CONTEXT & BACKGROUND:
  - [relevant background that explains why this matters]
```

---

## Step 2 — Assign Citation Numbers

Number each UNIQUE URL once. Use these numbers consistently across all three posts:

```
[1] Dawn News: https://dawn.com/...
[2] Geo TV: https://geo.tv/...
[3] Al Jazeera: https://aljazeera.com/...
```

---

## Step 3 — Identify the Hook

The hook is the **single most scroll-stopping element** from the story.

Hook selection checklist:
- [ ] Is it specific? (name + event + consequence, not vague)
- [ ] Is it new? (not something widely known already)
- [ ] Is it surprising or emotionally resonant?
- [ ] Does it create a question in the reader's mind?

The hook becomes the **first line** of your X post and the **first sentence** of your Instagram caption.

**Hook examples:**
- Weak: *"Pakistan faces economic challenges"*
- Strong: *"148 students killed in a strike on a girls' school — neither the US nor Israel acknowledged any attack"*

---

## Step 4 — Acknowledge Gaps

For each target that ended as ⚠️ Partial or ❌ Not Found:
- Do NOT guess or fill in with assumptions
- Use hedge language: *"according to reports"*, *"it remains unclear whether"*
- Mark the post note: `[unverified — could not confirm from credible source]`

---

## Step 5 — Self-Score Each Draft Post

After writing all three posts (see post-writing skill), score each one:

| Dimension | Score 5 | Score 3 | Score 1 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hook strength** | Instantly stops scroll, specific fact | Decent but generic opening | Generic or boring first line |
| **Factual density** | Specific names, dates, quotes, locations | Some specifics, some vague | No specifics — all vague |
| **Attribution** | Every key fact has `[1]` `[2]` `[3]` | Most facts attributed | Unattributed claims |

**Scoring rule:**
- Any post scoring ≤ 2 on any dimension → **rewrite that post only**
- Re-score after rewrite; accept after 2 attempts even if still weak
- All three posts must score ≥ 3 before saving to `/social_posts.md`
